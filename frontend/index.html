🔐 How to Create a Gmail App Password
1
Enable 2-Step Verification — Go to your Google Account at myaccount.google.com, then navigate to Security → 2-Step Verification and turn it on. App passwords are only available once this is active.
2
Open App Passwords — In the same Security section, scroll down to find App passwords (search for it if hidden). Click to open it.
3
Select App & Device — From the dropdowns, choose Mail as the app and Other (Custom name) as the device. Enter a name like "SpamGuard" to identify it later.
4
Generate the Password — Click Generate. Google will display a 16-character password in groups of 4 (e.g., xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx). Copy it immediately — it won't be shown again.
5
Paste it here — Enter your Gmail address above and paste the 16-character app password into the field below. Spaces are optional — both formats are accepted.
⚠️ Security note: App passwords grant access only to specific apps — not your full Google account. You can revoke them anytime from Google Account → Security → App passwords.
